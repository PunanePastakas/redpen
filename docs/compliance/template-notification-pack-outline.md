# Template Notification Pack Outline

Status: draft outline
Date: 2026-05-20

Purpose: ready-made text for schools to adapt for DPO review, parent/student
notification, and teacher onboarding. The school/controller must approve the
final text before use.

## 1. School DPO One-Pager

Subject: RedPen AI-assisted mathematics feedback pilot

Suggested text:

> RedPen is an AI-assisted mathematics feedback tool. Teachers upload a grading
> instruction and student work images. The system helps transcribe work, split it
> into tasks, draft feedback, suggest points, and propose editable annotations.
> The teacher reviews and confirms all feedback and point decisions before any
> student can see them.

Include:

- controller and processor roles;
- intended users and age group;
- data categories;
- lawful basis selected by school;
- DPIA/LIA location;
- data residency setup;
- sub-processors;
- retention policy;
- human oversight design;
- student/guardian rights route;
- support and incident contacts.

## 2. Parent/Guardian Notice

Suggested Estonian text:

> Meie kool kasutab matemaatika tööde tagasiside andmisel RedPen nimelist
> õpetajat abistavat digivahendit. Õpetaja võib laadida süsteemi õpilase
> matemaatika töö pildi ja hindamisjuhendi. Tehisintellekt aitab töö käsikirja
> lugeda, ülesandeid eristada ja koostada tagasiside mustandi. Lõpliku
> tagasiside ja punktid kinnitab alati õpetaja.
>
> Tehisintellekt ei tee õpilase kohta iseseisvat lõppotsust. Õpilane näeb ainult
> õpetaja poolt kinnitatud tagasisidet. Kui tagasiside tundub ebatäpne või
> ebaselge, saab õpilane või lapsevanem paluda õpetajalt selgitust või parandust.

Add school-specific:

- controller contact and DPO contact;
- what data is processed;
- retention period;
- student login/sharing method;
- rights request channel;
- whether points/grades are shown in RedPen.

## 3. Student Notice

Suggested Estonian text:

> RedPen aitab õpetajal sinu matemaatika tööd läbi vaadata. AI võib aidata
> käsikirja lugeda ja tagasiside mustandit teha, aga õpetaja kontrollib ja
> kinnitab tulemuse enne, kui sina seda näed. Kui midagi tundub vale, küsi
> õpetajalt selgitust.

## 4. Teacher Onboarding Note

Key points:

- Upload only work for your own class/test.
- Check student matches before analysis.
- Treat AI output as a draft.
- Confirm task points and annotations yourself.
- Do not share feedback until you have reviewed it.
- Do not upload real student work to demo, GitHub issues, screenshots, or public
  bug reports.
- Use synthetic examples when reporting bugs publicly.

## 5. Incident Template

Fields:

- date/time discovered;
- reporter;
- affected school/class/test;
- data involved;
- suspected cause;
- containment steps;
- whether raw images, feedback, account data, or audit logs were affected;
- whether data left approved EU systems;
- next action owner;
- DPO/controller notified at;
- supervisory authority/student/guardian notification assessment.
